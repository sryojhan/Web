About me

Hi, I’m Yojhan! I’m a game developer and software engineer with a passion for creating immersive experiences and solving technical challenges. I studied a degree in Game Development at the Universidad Complutense de Madrid and have over two years of professional experience at Blitworks, where I worked on ports, gameplay programming, and UI design for a variety of titles, including Parkitect, Everhood, and Pirates Outlaws. I love diving into indie games with innovative mechanics, both as a player and a developer, and I’m always eager to take on new creative projects. Let’s build something amazing together!

Parkitect

Build and manage your own amusement park in this complete tycoon full of content; a modern take on oldschool simulation games. Find the perfect balance between functionality, design, and fun. Take care of your clients' desires and needs, manage entry and attraction prices, decorate to increase immersion, build roller coasters part by part to maximize the fun, but make them functional and without making guests dizzy! Parkitect takes management to the maximum, allowing you to decide on every detail of your park.

I ported the game to next-gen consoles, ensuring optimal performance and a smooth user experience across all platforms. To enhance player interaction, I adapted the controls for gamepad use by designing and implementing a custom radial menu from scratch, which significantly improved usability. Additionally, I developed the in-game tutorial from the ground up, creating intuitive and engaging guidance for new players to understand the core mechanics.

For a DLC, I designed and built an effects editor that allowed players to coordinate and manage real-time park events with a user-friendly interface similar to a video editor. Throughout the project, I resolved numerous bugs and performance issues, enhancing the overall stability and polish of the game. I also collaborated closely with the team to ensure the seamless integration of new features and maintained a high standard of quality throughout the development process.

Everhood

Everhood Eternity Edition presents an unconventional and artistic fusion of rhythm-based gameplay, exploration with role-playing elements, and an abstract narrative. Its musical gameplay challenges players' reflexes, engaging them in rhythmic battles, and immerses them in a mesmerizing and otherworldly universe. The game takes inspiration from classic 16-bit era RPGs and modern titles like Undertale, Guitar Hero, and Deltarune. The narrative is subjective, leaving room for interpretation and inviting players to delve into its enigmatic world, questioning the nature of reality and contemplating deeper themes. The Eternity Edition not only ports the existing content but also enhances it with 16 new battles designed by the best level designers from the Everhood community, featuring music from 11 renowned composers. These new tracks are unlocked as players progress through the story, offering a real challenge for the most skilled.

I ported the game to next-gen consoles, adapting it for modern systems. This included creating new menus to integrate the additional content of the Eternity Edition, as well as adding new levels and implementing a ranking system. I also worked on optimizing controls, adapting the save system, and ensuring compatibility with console achievements. My efforts ensured a seamless and enhanced experience for players on consoles, maintaining the game's unique charm while adding new features and improvements.

Pirates outlaws

Developed by Fabled Game Studio, Pirates Outlaws is a roguelike card battler acclaimed by critics and players, where you navigate dangerous seas to defeat outlaws and their masters in tense combats. The game offers plenty of content and unlockables, diverse game modes, and a beautiful papercraft artstyle. Your expedition will be full of ambushes and challenges, so get ready, build your card deck, and plan deadly combos to become the most reputed pirate!

I ported the game to next-gen consoles, ensuring a smooth and optimized experience for players. This involved adapting the controls for gamepads, integrating a console-specific save system, and ensuring compatibility with achievements. Additionally, I focused on performance optimization to maintain smooth gameplay on new hardware, while preserving the game's unique charm and strategic depth. My work helped bring the thrilling world of Pirates Outlaws to a wider audience on modern platforms.
